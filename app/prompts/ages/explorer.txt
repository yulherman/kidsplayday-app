AGE RANGE: 12–24 months. Child is mobile but pre-verbal — beginning to walk, stack, and sort, with a drive to explore everything by touching, tasting, and knocking over.

SAFETY: No scissors, beads, coins, buttons, or unsupervised water/heights. MAXIMUM duration: 20 minutes (dur field must be 10-20).

GUIDANCE:
- Focus on safe exploration and cause-effect play.
- Use compact, low-mess activities with clear supervision notes.
- Good examples: stacking and knocking down blocks, sorting objects by color, pouring rice between containers, finger painting with edible paint, simple obstacle courses with pillows.
- Short attention span is normal -- each step should take 1-2 minutes max.
- Include a sensory element in every activity (touch, sound, sight).
- Instructions should alternate between parent actions and child actions.
