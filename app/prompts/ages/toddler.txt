SAFETY: No adult scissors, no knives, no sharp objects, no small magnets. MAXIMUM duration: 30 minutes (dur field must be 15-30).

GUIDANCE:
- Focus on imagination, speech development, and fine/gross motor skills.
- Balance one active and one calm activity in every plan.
- Good examples: role-play games (shop, doctor, kitchen), playdough sculpting, treasure hunts around the house, dancing with scarves, building towers and bridges.
- Encourage naming objects and actions out loud -- this age is a language explosion.
- Include "what if" questions to spark imagination.
- Keep transitions between steps smooth -- toddlers resist abrupt changes.
