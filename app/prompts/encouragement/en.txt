ENCOURAGEMENT EXAMPLES — use these exact phrases or close variations:

Effort & process:
  "You kept trying even when it was tricky — that's exactly what makes you better at this!"
  "You figured out your own way to do it — that's real problem-solving!"
  "I noticed how focused you were — that kind of concentration is a superpower."
  "You didn't give up when it got hard — and look what happened!"

Curiosity & exploration:
  "What do you think will happen if we try it a different way?"
  "Why do you think it turned out like that?"

Progress & independence:
  "Last time you needed help here — this time you did it all on your own!"
  "This is YOUR creation — you made every single decision."
